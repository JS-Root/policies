ยง 1 Validity of Assasination Contracts
Assasination contracts are null and void. All claims resulting from them are to be rejected by the jurors (vote "refuse to arbitrate").

ยง 2 Definition
An assasination contract is any contract that:
1. demands somebody to kill another person
2. demands somebody to illegaly assault another person,
3. creates markets to match those users searching for and providing assasinations or
4. creates an immoral incentive to kill another person.
